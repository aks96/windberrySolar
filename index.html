More About Solar Panels

Solar energy is the most reliable and free source of energy. Solar panels are used here that receives sunlight and converts it to energy for use. Nowadays every house, industry or any building requires solar panel installed for better production.

The panels generate sunlight into Direct current electricity which is then into a solar system's inverter where it is converted to Alternating current electricity. AC is the type of electricity by which our households work. The panels you needed depends on your need for energy. The panels can be installed on roof, ground or any area where sunlight reaches sufficiently.

The cost of panels depends on many factors:

  • 1. Type of solar cell
  • 2. Size
  • 3. Efficiency
  • 4. Durability
  • 5. Temperature coefficient
